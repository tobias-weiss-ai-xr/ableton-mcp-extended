### 2. Load Instruments

Once recording is complete:

**Recommended Instruments:**
- **Dub Bass:** Sub-bass synth (Operator, Serum, Subtractor)
- **Drums:** Drum rack or individual drum sounds
- **Atmosphere:** Pad synth with heavy reverb
- **Dub Melody:** Lead synth or pluck with delay
- **FX:** Sampler with sweeps and impacts
- **Percussion:** Percussion sounds (shaker, cabasa, etc.)

### 3. Add Effects (Essential for Dub)

**Required Effects:**
- **Reverb:** Heavy reverb on atmosphere, melody, FX
- **Delay:** Dub delays on bass, melody, drums (use sends)
- **Filters:** Low-pass filters for depth
- **Compression:** Glue drum elements together
- **Sidechain:** Duck bass against kick

**Automation Ideas:**
- Filter sweeps on buildups
- Reverb sends on drops
- Delay time changes for variation
- Volume automation for dynamics
- Pan automation for stereo width

### 4. Mixing

**Track Levels:**
- **Bass:** Heavy compression, EQ (boost 60-120Hz)
- **Kick:** Punchy, prominent, no competition with bass
- **Snare:** Crisp, add room reverb
- **Atmosphere:** Wide stereo, low volume (-20dB)
- **Melody:** Add delay and reverb, moderate volume
- **FX:** Automate for dramatic effects

### 5. Export

**Best Quality Settings:**
- **Rendered Track:** Master
- **Sample Rate:** 48000 Hz
- **Bit Depth:** 24-bit or 32-bit float
- **File Type:** WAV (for production) or MP3 (for sharing)
- **Normalization:** Off (handle levels manually)

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### Recording Issues

**Can't start recording?**
- Check Ableton is in Session View (TAB key)
- Verify Record button is enabled (not greyed out)
- Check disk has space for recording

**Clips not playing?**
- Verify track volume is up
- Check clips are not muted
- Ensure MIDI is armed if needed

**Sections not switching automatically?**
- Check `record_dub_song_now.py` is running
- Verify Ableton is responsive
- Try manual switching with `switch_section.py`

### Audio Issues

**No sound?**
- Check speakers are connected
- Verify Master volume is up
- Confirm tracks are armed for MIDI

**Clipping/distortion?**
- Lower track volumes
- Check compression settings
- Reduce input levels
